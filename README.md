# Cadbury-Dashboard
**Cadbury Sales & Business Performance Dashboard
Project Overview**

The Cadbury Sales & Business Performance Dashboard is an end-to-end Power BI solution designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, distribution efficiency, and promotional effectiveness. The dashboard provides management with a centralized view of key business metrics and enables data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations and drill-through analysis.

Built using historical sales and operational data, the report transforms raw transactional information into actionable insights across multiple business functions, helping stakeholders identify growth opportunities, monitor performance, and improve strategic planning.

**Business Objective**

The primary objective of this dashboard is to provide a comprehensive view of Cadbury's commercial performance by analyzing:

Revenue and sales trends
Product performance and profitability
Customer purchasing behavior
Distribution and fulfillment effectiveness
Promotional campaign impact
Target achievement and operational KPIs

The dashboard enables executives and business analysts to track performance across different dimensions and quickly identify areas requiring attention.

**Dashboard Pages
Executive Summary**

Provides a high-level overview of business performance through KPI cards, revenue metrics, profitability indicators, and trend analysis.

**Sales Analysis**

Analyzes sales performance across time periods, regions, and business segments to identify growth patterns and seasonal trends.

**Product Analysis**

Evaluates product-wise sales contribution, profitability, volume performance, and product portfolio effectiveness.

**Customer Analysis**

Examines customer purchasing behavior, revenue contribution, and customer segmentation insights.

**Distribution Analysis**

Monitors distribution performance, fulfillment rates, lead times, and operational efficiency across the supply chain.

**Promotion Analysis**

Measures the effectiveness of promotional activities, discounts, and their impact on sales and profitability.

**Drill-Through Analysis**

Allows users to investigate specific products, customers, or business segments at a granular level.

**Interactive Filters & Slicers**

Provides dynamic filtering capabilities for customized analysis and reporting.

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
Total Sales, Net Sales,Gross Profit, Gross Margin %, Total Units Sold, Average Discount %, Return Rate %,Actual vs Target Performance, Forecast Accuracy, Fulfillment Rate, Average Lead Time, Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth, Year-to-Date (YTD) Sales, Quarter-to-Date (QTD) Sales, Tools & Technologies, Power BI Desktop – Dashboard development and visualization, Power Query – Data transformation and preparation, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI and business metric calculations, Microsoft Excel – Source data management, Star Schema Data Model – Optimized reporting structure, Interactive Drill-Through & Tooltips – Enhanced user experience

**Business Value**

**This dashboard helps stakeholders:**

Monitor business performance in real time
Identify high-performing products and customers
Evaluate promotional effectiveness
Improve operational efficiency
Track performance against targets
Support strategic and data-driven decision-making
